Oranjestad University

Aruba's Newest University

Apply now to study at Aruba's newest university! Named after and located in Aruba's capital city, this undergraduate university provides students resources to further their knowledges and careers/

Programs

Aruba's OU provides a very specialized set of programs to better support students with interests in ecological studies. Our programs include:

  • Marine Biology
  • Ecology
  • Aquaculture
  • Aquatic Environmental Management
  • and more!

Application Materials

OU's application process is simplified, only requiring the following for application:

  • Proof of identity (Driver's license, birth certificate, or passport)
  • High school diploma, GED, or equivalent completion of primary education
  • Transcripts from primary education

Additional test scores and other supplemental materials are welcomed, but not required.

Deadlines

All programs offered by OU have the same deadlines for application. Note that OU only admits new or transfer students in the fall semester. There are three admission timelines:

  • Early Admission: October 15th
  • Primary Admission (Requires Approval): December 31st
  • Regular Admission: April 29th

No applications for the upcoming fall semester will be accepted after April 29th.
